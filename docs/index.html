Press a shortcut, speak, and have your words appear in any text field. Whisper or Parakeet, running entirely on your own computer. Free and open source.

Download View on GitHub Release notes

Built for focus.

Simple, private, keyboard-driven. Escribbo does one thing and stays out of the way.

Fully offline

No audio leaves your machine. Whisper and Parakeet run locally with GPU acceleration when available.

Keyboard-first

Global shortcuts for toggle, push-to-talk, and post-processing. Works across apps on Windows, macOS, and Linux.

Real-time speeds

Parakeet V3 hits ~5× real-time on a mid-range CPU. Whisper variants scale from lean to large with GPU offload.

Material You UI

Pick any seed color; the entire interface and recording overlay retint to match. Light and dark themes included.

CLI-friendly

Start hidden, skip the tray, toggle via CLI flags, or bind SIGUSR1/2 on Linux. Fits any workflow.

Open source, MIT

A friendly fork of Handy with a fresh coat of paint. Fork it, hack it, ship it.
